astrothesis

astrothesis is a creative science space where physics, astronomy and cosmic ideas come together. Our goal is to allow aspiring scientists to have a platform to connect and feed curiosity. Navigate the galaxy, unravel cosmic mysteries, and traverse the science behind the stars. Every post is a new discovery - are you ready to explore?

I’m Taseefah, and I created astrothesis because science—especially astrophysics—can often feel inaccessible. There’s so much knowledge online, but it’s scattered or difficult to find. This platform aims to bridge that gap: to give students direction, inspire curiosity, and allow scientists to connect and collaborate. After all, astrophysics is the future, and everyone should have the opportunity to explore it.

As a student myself, I want to bring science closer to the community. While this starts as a humble website, I hope to eventually introduce it to schools and use it as a platform to share not only my own discoveries but also other exciting new developments in science through joint blogs with notable people in the field.
